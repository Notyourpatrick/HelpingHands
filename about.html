Who We Are

HelpingHands is a non-profit organization committed to supporting specially-abled children and underprivileged communities by providing access to education, skill development, and emotional well-being resources.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower individuals through education, guidance, and opportunities for a better future.

Our Vision

A world where every individual has equal access to opportunities regardless of their circumstances.
